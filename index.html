— about me

I am a Senior Postdoctoral Researcher at MIT Senseable City Lab, and Global Labs Lead in our labs in Amsterdam, Dubai, and Rio. My work focuses on how technology, design, and data science can enhance urban environments.

My research has been published in leading journals like Nature Computational Science, Nature Water, and Nature Cities, highlighting innovative solutions for global urban challenges.

I aim to identify context-specific problems and create scalable, globally applicable solutions with a data-driven and multidisciplinary approach.
